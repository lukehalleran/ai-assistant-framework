knowledge/synthesis_filter.py (520 lines)
  - 8-stage async filter pipeline for graph walk candidates:
    Stage 0: Text sanity (min tokens, verb detection, repetition filter)
    Stage 1: Domain crossing (require >=2 distinct domains)
    Stage 2: Semantic distance (endpoint distance in [0.20, 0.90])
    Stage 3: External novelty (wiki corpus search, reject if similarity > 0.80)
    Stage 4: Internal novelty (synthesis memory dedup, convergence signal)
    Stage 5: Coherence judge (LLM rates INVALID/WEAK/MODERATE/STRONG)
    Stage 6: Composite score (weighted >=0.40: coherence+novelty+distance+structural)
    Stage 7: Storage/convergence tracking
