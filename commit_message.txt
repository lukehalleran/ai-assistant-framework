feat: Multi-signal novelty scoring, two-pass coherence judge, operational docs

Replaces single-signal novelty gate with 4-signal composite, rewrites the
coherence judge prompt for structural isomorphism detection, adds factual
skeptic second pass, and creates standalone operational guides for the
synthesis filter and memory system.

Calibrated against 54 labeled candidates with live Sonnet 4.5 + GPT-4o-mini.
Final metrics (Sonnet 4.5): Precision=90.9%, Recall=100%, F1=95.2%.
