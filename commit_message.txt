Replaces old TP/FP/FN grading with a two-layer system: 3 binary
screening questions (changes thinking? / mechanism real? / heard before?)
plus a 1-5 gut-feel slider. Binary screening captures fast honest signal
for classifier training; slider captures the 3/4 boundary nuance.
