## Temporal-Aware Recency Decay

TEMPORAL_RECALL queries ("What happened last week?") now surface time-relevant
memories instead of burying them under standard exponential decay.

**Changes:** `core/intent_classifier.py`, `memory/memory_scorer.py`
- IntentClassifier.classify() extracts temporal anchor via extract_temporal_window()
- Adds _temporal_anchor_hours to weight_overrides (e.g., "last week" → 168h)
- MemoryScorer.rank_memories() reshapes decay curve:
  - Within window: gentle decay (1.0 → 0.7)
  - Outside window: standard decay from 0.7 baseline
- Temporal anchor takes priority over time_manager and fallback paths
